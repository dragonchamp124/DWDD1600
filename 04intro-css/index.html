Mono Green

Play style

Green is a very aggresive color and focuses on large creatures and high damage output. Green is a very basic color on its own not using lots of interaction outside of combat tricks and fight spells.

Goals

  1. Green wants to ramp in the early game to be able to play the expensive powerhouses late game it'll do this using the large variety of spells that will search for lands
  2. Green will either have a huge army of creatures, very large and scary creatures, or a combination of both
